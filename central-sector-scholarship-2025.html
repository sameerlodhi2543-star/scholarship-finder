Central Sector Scholarship 2025 (CSS)

12th board me achhe marks wale college students ke liye central government ki Central Sector Scholarship (CSS). Yaha aapko simple language me eligibility, income limit, amount aur apply steps milenge.

Ye official website nahi hai. Detailed notification aur final dates ke liye hamesha National Scholarship Portal (NSP) par check karein.

Go to National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

1. Central Sector Scholarship 2025 – Overview

  • Scheme: Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College & University Students
  • Authority: Government of India
  • Level: Graduation (UG) & Post‑Graduation (PG)
  • Official Portal: scholarships.gov.in (NSP)

2. Important Dates (Expected) – 2025

Dates har saal thodi change ho sakti hain. Neeche 2025 ke liye approx idea diya gaya hai. Final dates ke liye NSP portal par notice dekhein.

Activity Tentative Time
Online application start August–September 2025
Last date for new application October–November 2025
Institute verification November–December 2025

3. Eligibility – Kaun apply kar sakta hai?

Basic conditions

  • Student India ka citizen ho.
  • 12th board (CBSE/State board) me top meritorious students ke beech honi chahiye rank / percentage.
  • Student recognized college / university me regular UG ya PG course me admitted ho.
  • Family income ek fixed limit tak (usually around ₹8 lakh per year; latest notification check karein).

Marks criteria (general idea)

  • 12th board exam me high percentage (for example 80% ya uske aas‑paas; exact cut‑off har board ke liye alag hota hai).
  • Reservation category ke liye relaxations ho sakti hain.

4. Scholarship Amount (Approx)

Amount year ke hisaab se change ho sakta hai. Common pattern:

  • UG (Graduation): per year fixed amount (e.g. ₹10,000–₹12,000 ke aas‑paas).
  • PG (Post‑Graduation): thoda zyada amount (e.g. ₹20,000 per year ke aas‑paas).
  • Amount directly bank account me DBT ke through aata hai (Aadhaar linked account).

Exact rate aur latest revision ke liye official guidelines / NSP document padhein.

5. Required Documents

  • Aadhaar card
  • 12th board marksheet
  • Admission proof (UG/PG course me entry)
  • Fee receipt / college ID card
  • Bank passbook (student ke naam par)
  • Income certificate (competent authority se)
  • Caste certificate (agar applicable ho)
  • Recent passport size photo

6. Central Sector Scholarship 2025 – Apply Online Steps

  1. NSP portal open karein: scholarships.gov.in
  2. New Registration: “New Registration” pe click karke guidelines padhein aur accept karein.
  3. Student registration form fill: Basic details, bank details, Aadhaar etc. fill karke register karein.
  4. Login: Application ID / username ke saath login karein.
  5. Application form: Scheme list me se “Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students” select karein aur form fill karein.
  6. Documents upload: Required documents ki scanned copy upload karein.
  7. Final submit: Form preview check karein, galti ho to edit karein, fir final submit karein.
  8. Institute verification: College/institute portal ke through application verify karega – is stage ka status regular check karein.

7. FAQs – Central Sector Scholarship 2025

Q. Kya ye scholarship sab boards ke students ke liye hai?
Ans. Haan, CBSE, ICSE aur recognized state boards – sabke liye alag‑alag merit list banti hai.

Q. Kya private college ke students apply kar sakte hain?
Ans. Agar college recognized / accredited hai aur govt. guideline ke hisaab se eligible course chala raha hai to usually allowed hota hai.

Q. Kya ek saal miss hone par agle saal renew ho sakta hai?
Ans. Renewal rules strict hote hain (minimum marks + regular promotion). Renewal miss ho jaye to dobara fresh apply karne ka option depend karta hai official rules par.
