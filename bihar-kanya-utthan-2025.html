Mukhyamantri Kanya Utthan Yojana 2025 (Graduation)

Bihar me graduation complete kar chuki unmarried ladkiyon ke liye ek‑baar ka financial assistance.

Exact amount, last date aur conditions ke liye hamesha ekalyan.bih.nic.in portal par official notification dekhein.

Go to Bihar e‑Kalyan Portal

1. Basic Details

  • State: Bihar
  • Target group: Graduation pass girls (B.A./B.Sc./B.Com. etc.)
  • Type: Ek baar ka incentive / assistance (scholarship jaisa support)

2. Eligibility (General Idea)

  • Applicant Bihar ka resident ho.
  • Girl student ne recognized university se graduation pass kiya ho.
  • Mostly unmarried ladkiyon ke liye scheme hoti hai (marital status rule check karein).
  • Graduation passing year aur percentage ke liye portal par condition hoti hai.

3. Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card
  • Bihar domicile / residence certificate
  • Graduation marksheet & degree certificate
  • Bank passbook (applicant ke naam par)
  • Recent photo
  • Unmarried certificate / self declaration (agar required ho)

4. Apply Process (Online)

  1. e‑Kalyan website open karein – ekalyan.bih.nic.in
  2. Kanya Utthan yojana / graduation girls section select karein.
  3. Registration karein (agar required ho) aur login karein.
  4. Application form me personal, academic & bank details fill karein.
  5. Required documents ki scanned copy upload karein.
  6. Final submit karke application number note kar lein, status portal par check karte rahein.
