Lakshmir Bhandar Scheme 2025

West Bengal government ki women income support scheme, jisme eligible mahilaon ko har mahine fixed rashi di jati hai.

Different categories (SC/ST/General) ke liye alag amounts ho sakte hain – latest GO ya portal par check karein.

West Bengal Govt Schemes Page

1. Who is Targeted?

  • Women head of family / household.
  • Economically weaker families, SC/ST/General category as per rules.

2. General Eligibility

  • Applicant West Bengal ka permanent resident ho.
  • Age & income criteria state guideline ke hisaab se match kare.
  • Doosri similar pension / high government job waghaira ho to exclude ho sakta hai.

3. Apply / Benefit Receive

Application mostly offline+online process ke through hoti hai (Duare Sarkar camps / local offices ke through). Approved applicants ke account me monthly payment DBT se aata hai.
