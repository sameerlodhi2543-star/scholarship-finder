AICTE Pragati Scholarship 2025

Engineering / diploma / technical degree me admission lene wali girl students ke liye AICTE ki special scholarship scheme.

Exact seat count, amount aur dates ke liye AICTE ki official website par latest advertisement check karein.

Official Pragati Portal

1. Overview

  • Scheme: Pragati Scholarship for Girl Students
  • Authority: AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education)
  • Level: Diploma / Degree level technical courses (engineering, pharmacy, architecture etc.)

2. Eligibility (Basic)

  • Girl student ho (female candidate).
  • AICTE approved institution / course me admission liya ho (1st year).
  • Family income usually ₹8 lakh/year se kam (latest rule dekh lein).
  • Ek family se fixed number of girls hi eligible hoti hain (normally 2).

3. Scholarship Benefits (Approx)

  • Per year fixed tuition fee support (e.g. up to ₹50,000 per year tak – exact amount notification me).
  • Additional allowance (books, equipment, etc.) bhi diya ja sakta hai.

4. Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card
  • 10th & 12th / diploma previous marksheets
  • Admission letter & fee receipt
  • AICTE approved institution ka proof
  • Income certificate
  • Bank passbook (student ke naam par)
  • Caste certificate (agar applicable ho)

5. Apply Online Steps

  1. Official AICTE Pragati portal open karein – aicte-pragati-saksham-gov.in
  2. “Register” option se naya account banayein.
  3. Login karke Pragati Scholarship scheme select karein.
  4. Application form me personal, academic & institute details fill karein.
  5. Required documents upload karein aur form submit karein.
  6. Institute level & AICTE level verification ke status ko portal par check karte rahein.
