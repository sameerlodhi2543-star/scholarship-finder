Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana 2025

Gareeb parivaron ki mahila ke naam par LPG gas connection dene ki scheme, taaki chulha ke dhue se health problems kam ho aur clean fuel use ho.

Latest phase ki eligibility aur subsidy structure ke liye official Ujjwala portal / oil marketing company ki website check karein.

PM Ujjwala Official Website

1. Basic Eligibility (common points)

  • Woman applicant BPL / poor household se ho (list / ration card ke hisaab se).
  • Family ke paas pehle se LPG connection na ho.
  • Age aur identification documents complete ho (Aadhaar, ration card, etc.).

2. Benefits

  • New LPG connection (connection charge waise hi ya subsidised).
  • First cylinder / stove ke liye subsidy ya EMI type support (phase ke hisaab se).

3. Apply Process (General)

  1. Nearest LPG distributor (IOCL/HPCL/BPCL) se form lena ya online portal check karna.
  2. Form me applicant details, address, KYC details fill karna.
  3. Required documents attach karke distributor ke paas jama karna.
  4. Approval ke baad LPG connection allocate hota hai.
