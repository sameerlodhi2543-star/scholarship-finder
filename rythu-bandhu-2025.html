Rythu Bandhu Scheme 2025

Telangana government ki scheme jisme har cropping season me kisanon ko per acre fixed amount diya jata hai taaki beej, khaad, pesticide ka kharcha nikal sake.

Exact amount, paid acres aur schedule ke liye official Rythu Bandhu portal / state agriculture website check karein.

Rythu Bandhu Official Portal

1. Scheme Summary

  • Eligibility mainly Telangana ke landholding farmers ke liye.
  • Per acre input assistance (each crop season) directly bank account me.

2. Basic Eligibility

  • Farmer ke naam par Telangana me registered agricultural land ho.
  • Land details Dharani / state land record system me update ho.
  • Tenant farmers ke liye alag rules / discussions state level par chal sakte hain – latest update dekhna zaroori hai.

3. How to Check Rythu Bandhu Payment Status

  1. Portal open karein – rythubandhu.telangana.gov.in
  2. Farmer-wise details / cheque distribution report section me jaayein.
  3. District, mandal, village select karke apna naam/khata verify karein.
