Jagananna Amma Vodi Scheme 2025

AP me poor families ki mothers/guardians ko har saal education ke liye financial assistance di jati hai, taaki bachche school/college chhod na dein.

Eligibility list, amount aur year-wise guidelines ke liye official Amma Vodi website / state portal dekhna zaroori hai.

Amma Vodi Official Portal

1. Scheme Objective

Poor families ko direct financial help dekar unke bachchon ko school se drop‑out hone se bachana, aur unko Class 12 / higher education tak padhai continue karne ke liye encourage karna.

2. Basic Eligibility (general)

  • Mother/guardian Andhra Pradesh ka resident ho.
  • Bachcha recognized school/college me regular student ho.
  • Family income aur other criteria government ke norms ke according ho (white ration card etc.).

3. Application / Beneficiary List

  1. Official portal par district/school wise list check ki ja sakti hai.
  2. Usually data school education department ke through verify hota hai; individual online form har saal jaroori ho bhi sakta hai, nahi bhi – notification dekhna hoga.
