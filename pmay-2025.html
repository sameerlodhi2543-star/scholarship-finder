Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana 2025 (PMAY)

Shehri (Urban) aur Gramin (Rural) gareeb parivaron ko pakka ghar dene ke liye sarkari yojana. Ghar banane / kharidne / sudharne par subsidy ya financial help.

PMAY ke rules city/state ke hisaab se change ho sakte hain. Official portals se latest details zaroor check karein.

PMAY (Urban) Official Portal

1. PM Awas Yojana – Main Types

  • PMAY‑Urban (PMAY‑U) – shehri areas ke liye.
  • PMAY‑Gramin (PMAY‑G) – gaon / rural areas ke liye.
  • Alag‑alag component: ghar construction, upgradation, CLSS (home loan interest subsidy) etc.

2. General Eligibility (simple idea)

  • Beneficiary ke paas pakka ghar nahi hona chahiye (ya bahut kaccha / jhuggi type house ho).
  • Family income EWS/LIG/MIG category ke hisaab se fixed limit ke andar ho.
  • Urban ke liye mostly city body ke through survey/list banti hai; gramin ke liye Socio Economic Caste Census (SECC) data use hota hai.

3. Benefits (short)

  • New house construction ke liye grant/subsidy.
  • Existing house improvement/extension ke liye financial help.
  • CLSS ke through home loan par interest subsidy (Urban component).

4. Apply / Status – Kahan dekhna hai?

  • PMAY‑Urban: pmaymis.gov.in – search beneficiary / application status / CLSS info.
  • PMAY‑Gramin: pmayg.nic.in – rural beneficiary list, status, etc.
  • State govt. websites par bhi additional guidelines aur list mil sakti hai.
