INSPIRE Scholarship 2025 – SHE

Department of Science & Technology (DST) ki scholarship jo science stream ke top students ko BSc/BS/Integrated MSc courses ke liye di jati hai.

Eligibility mainly board marks + rank par based hoti hai. Exact cut‑off list har saal INSPIRE ki site par upload hoti hai.

INSPIRE Online Portal

1. Overview

  • Scheme: INSPIRE – Scholarship for Higher Education (SHE)
  • Authority: DST, Government of India
  • Target: Science stream se 12th pass top students

2. Eligibility – Main Points

  • 12th board exam me science subjects ke saath high marks (top percentile) lekar pass kiya ho.
  • BSc / BS / Integrated MSc type science course me admission liya ho (engineering nahi).
  • College / institute recognized ho.

Exact board‑wise percentage / rank cut‑off har saal alag PDF me diya jata hai – INSPIRE website par “SHE Cut‑off” document check karein.

3. Scholarship Benefits

  • Har saal fixed scholarship amount (monthly stipend + contingency grant) – exact figure notification me.
  • UG/PG course ki normal duration tak support mil sakta hai (conditions ke saath).

4. Documents Required (example list)

  • Aadhaar card
  • 12th marksheet & certificate
  • Rank/percentile proof (agar required ho)
  • Admission letter / ID card
  • Bank passbook (student ke naam par)
  • Caste / category certificate (agar applicable)
  • Income certificate (agar required ho)

5. Apply Process (Online)

  1. Portal kholein – online-inspire.gov.in
  2. “Register” section se new user registration karein.
  3. Login karke INSPIRE–SHE scholarship scheme select karein.
  4. Application form me personal, academic & course details fill karein.
  5. Required documents upload karein (clear scanned copies).
  6. Form submit karne ke baad application ID note kar lein aur status ko portal par track karein.
