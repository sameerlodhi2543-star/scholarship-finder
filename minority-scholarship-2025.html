Minority Scholarship 2025 – Pre & Post Matric

Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, Parsi students ke liye central government ki important scholarships – pre‑matric (Class 9–10) aur post‑matric (11–12, UG, PG).

Yaha basic overview hai. Exact dates, income limit aur amount ke liye hamesha National Scholarship Portal (NSP) par latest notification dekhein.

Go to NSP – Minority Scholarships

1. Kaun‑kaun si schemes aati hain?

  • Pre‑Matric Minority Scholarship 2025 – Class 9 & 10 ke liye.
  • Post‑Matric Minority Scholarship 2025 – 11–12, UG, PG, professional courses ke liye.
  • Merit‑cum‑Means Scholarship – professional / technical UG & PG courses ke liye.

2. Basic Eligibility

  • Student notified minority community se belong karta ho (Muslim / Christian / Sikh / Buddhist / Jain / Parsi).
  • Student recognized school / college me regular padhai kar raha ho.
  • Family income usually ₹1 lakh se ₹2.5 lakh ke beech (scheme ke hisaab se).
  • Previous class me minimum required percentage (e.g. 50%+). Exact percentage notification me diya hota hai.

3. Scholarship Amount (Approx)

  • Pre‑Matric: tuition fee + maintenance allowance per month/year.
  • Post‑Matric: course fee + hostel/day scholar ke liye alag‑alag maintenance amount.
  • Merit‑cum‑Means: professional course fee ka bada hissa + maintenance.

Amount course aur state ke hisaab se change ho sakta hai – latest guideline NSP par check karein.

4. Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card
  • Minority community certificate (agar required ho)
  • Income certificate
  • Domicile / residence proof
  • Last exam ki marksheet
  • Fee receipt / admission proof
  • Bank passbook (student ke naam par)
  • Passport size photo

5. Apply Online – NSP ke through

  1. NSP portal open karein – scholarships.gov.in
  2. “New Registration” select karke guidelines padhein.
  3. Student registration form fill karein (basic + bank details).
  4. Login karein aur scheme list me se apni minority scheme select karein.
  5. Application form complete karein, documents upload karein.
  6. Final submit ke baad form ka print nikal lein, institute verification ka status NSP par dekhte rahen.
