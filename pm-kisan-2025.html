PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana 2025

Chhote kisanon ke liye central government ki income support scheme. Har saal fixed rashi kisan ke bank account me direct aati hai.

Yaha sirf general information hai. Exact amount, installment dates aur rules ke liye pmkisan.gov.in par latest update dekhein.

Go to PM Kisan Official Website

1. PM Kisan 2025 – Basic Overview

  • Scheme: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM‑Kisan)
  • Target: Small & marginal farmers
  • Benefit: Har eligible kisan ko har saal kist me fixed rashi (DBT).
  • Official Portal: pmkisan.gov.in

2. Eligibility – Kaun kisan PM Kisan ke liye eligible hai?

  • Applicant India ka kisan ho, apni ya joint naam par cultivable land ho.
  • Land record state ke portal ya revenue records me updated ho.
  • Kuch categories (income tax payer, high post govt employees, institutional land holders, etc.) usually exclude hote hain.

Exact rules state ke land record system aur central guideline ke hisaab se decide hote hain – portal par latest FAQ zaroor padhein.

3. Kitni rashi milti hai? (General idea)

  • Har eligible farmer ko saal bhar me fixed amount teen bar (installments) me milti hai.
  • Rashi direct bank account me DBT ke through credit hoti hai.
  • Installment dates aur amount government notification ke hisaab se update hote rehte hain.

4. PM Kisan Registration – Basic Steps

  1. Website kholein – pmkisan.gov.in
  2. “Farmer Corner” section me New Farmer Registration option select karein.
  3. State, Aadhaar number / mobile number enter karke verification karein.
  4. Apni personal details, land details, bank details sahi‑sahi fill karein.
  5. Form submit karein – registration number note karein.
  6. State level approval ke baad hi installment start hoti hai.

5. PM Kisan Status 2025 kaise check karein?

  1. Portal par “Farmer Corner” me Beneficiary Status option open karein.
  2. Aadhaar number / account number / mobile number enter karein.
  3. Captcha fill karke submit karein.
  4. Screen par aapka status dikhega – approved / pending / rejected, aur latest installment details.
