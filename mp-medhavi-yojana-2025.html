Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana 2025 (MMVY)

MP ke 12th me high score lane wale students ko engineering, medical, law aur other graduation courses ki fees ke liye yeh scheme support deti hai.

Exact marks cutoff, income limit aur eligible courses ke liye scholarshipportal.mp.nic.in par latest guideline dekhein.

MP Scholarship Portal

1. Basic Eligibility

  • Student Madhya Pradesh ka domicile ho.
  • 12th board exam me minimum required percentage (e.g. 70%+ state board, 85%+ CBSE/ICSE – actual rule dekhna zaroori).
  • Family income ek fixed limit se kam (approx ₹6 lakh/year; latest notification confirm karein).
  • Recognized college / university me eligible graduation course me admission liya ho.

2. Covered Courses (general idea)

  • Engineering (B.E./B.Tech)
  • Medical (MBBS) – government / selected colleges
  • Law, B.A., B.Sc., B.Com., other UG courses (as per official list)

3. Benefits

  • Eligible students ki tuition fee ka full / partial reimbursement government ke taraf se.
  • Government / selected private institutions ke liye different fee rules ho sakte hain.

4. Documents Required

  • Aadhaar & domicile certificate
  • 12th marksheet
  • Entrance exam scorecard (engineering/medical ke liye, agar required ho)
  • Admission letter & fee details
  • Income certificate
  • Bank passbook (student ke naam par)
  • Caste certificate (agar applicable ho)

5. Apply Process

  1. Portal open karein – scholarshipportal.mp.nic.in
  2. MMVY scheme select karein.
  3. Registration / login complete karein.
  4. Application form bhar ke documents upload karein.
  5. Institute verification aur department approval ka status portal pe track karein.
