NMMS Scholarship 2025 – National Means‑cum‑Merit

Economically weaker lekin meritorious school students ke liye important scheme. Class 8 me exam, phir 9 se 12 tak scholarship mil sakti hai.

Yaha basic guidance hai. Exact dates, exam pattern aur state‑wise notice ke liye apne state education department aur official website check karein.

Ministry of Education – NMMS Info

1. NMMS Scholarship 2025 – Overview

  • Full Name: National Means‑cum‑Merit Scholarship (NMMS)
  • Level: Class 8 ke baad 9–12 tak support
  • Objective: Economically weak par bright students ko school chhodne se rokna

2. Eligibility – Basic Points

  • Student government / government‑aided / local body school me padh raha ho.
  • Usually Class 8 ke students NMMS exam ke liye apply karte hain.
  • Family income approx ₹3,50,000 per year se kam (exact limit notification me).
  • Minimum required percentage Class 7/8 me (e.g. 55–60% ke aas‑paas).

3. Scholarship Amount

General structure:

  • Class 9 se 12 tak fixed amount per month / per year (central government decide karti hai).
  • Payment DBT ke through student ke bank account me jaata hai (Aadhaar se linked).

4. NMMS Exam Pattern (general idea)

  • Test usually do part me hota hai – MAT (Mental Ability Test) & SAT (Scholastic Aptitude Test).
  • Objective type questions, OMR sheet par.
  • Subjects: Maths, Science, Social Science, Reasoning etc.
  • Har state ka exact pattern, number of questions aur qualifying marks thoda differ kar sakta hai.

5. NMMS 2025 – Apply Process (State‑wise)

NMMS forms mostly state education department ya state scholarship portal par aate hain. Common steps:

  1. Apne state education website / SCERT website par “NMMS 2025” notification search karein.
  2. State notification download karke dates, eligibility aur pattern padhein.
  3. Online form fill karein (ya school through offline form, state instructions ke hisaab se).
  4. School/teacher se form verify karwa kar exam ke liye admit card download karein.

6. Tips for Students

  • Reasoning, Maths aur basic Science/SST ke concept strong karein.
  • Previous year NMMS papers aur sample questions solve karein.
  • Regular practice karen – kyunki exam time bound hota hai.
