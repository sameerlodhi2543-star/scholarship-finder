Maharashtra Open Merit Scholarship 2025

Maharashtra board ke SSC (10th) aur HSC (12th) exams me high percentage lane wale students ke liye state ki open merit scholarship schemes.

Latest eligibility & application process ke liye mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in ya education department ke portal par notice dekhein.

MahaDBT Scholarship Portal

1. Basic Idea

  • 10th / 12th me high percentage wale students ko further education ke liye incentive.
  • Different categories ke liye alag‑alag open merit schemes ho sakti hain.

2. Eligibility (general)

  • Maharashtra state board (SSC/HSC) se exam pass kiya ho.
  • Minimum required marks (e.g. 75%+ ya top rank) – exact cutoff notification me.
  • Further education me admission liya ho (11th/12th/UG etc.).

3. Documents Required

  • Aadhaar & domicile certificate
  • SSC/HSC marksheet
  • Admission proof (next class / course)
  • Bank passbook
  • Caste certificate (agar reservation based scheme ho)

4. Apply Process

  1. MahaDBT portal open karein – mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in
  2. New registration karein / login karein.
  3. Open Merit related scholarship scheme select karein.
  4. Online form fill karke documents upload karein.
  5. Institute verification aur approval status portal se track karein.
